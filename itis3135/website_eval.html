Website Evaluations

  • Contrast
  • Repetition
  • Alignment
  • Proximity

Unseen Studio

Unseen Studio has a great design according to C.R.A.P Principles. They add quite a lot of visual interests with the way they incorporated images and the mouse to work together. The website is very interactive as well.

  • C - The website uses a dark background with white text, ensuring readability. It also employs contrast in font size and weight to highlight important information.
  • R - Design elements like the navigation menu remain consistent across pages, maintaining a cohesive user experience.
  • A - Elements are well-aligned both horizontally and vertically, creating a clean layout and clear hierarchy of information.
  • P - Related elements are grouped closely together, enhancing readability and allowing users to quickly locate information.


Craigslist

Craigslist has a very bad design according to C.R.A.P Principles. The design has many links cramped together in the center in right blue font. There is very little contrast in the overall colors that website is using.

  • C - The website lacks optimal contrast between text and background, impacting readability. Design elements also lack contrast, leading to a uniform appearance.
  • R - While some layout structure repeats across listings, there's inconsistency in design elements throughout different sections/pages.
  • A - Elements often lack consistent alignment, affecting visual appeal and readability.
  • P - Information can feel cluttered due to poor proximity between elements, making it difficult for users to quickly find what they need.
